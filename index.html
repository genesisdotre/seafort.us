Financials

The asking price is £550k. The refurbishment is another £200k. £50k requires as a operation capital. Total value of the project: £750k that equals to roughly $1m.

Quick look at commercial mortgages - usually 70% loan to value is allowed - that means approximately £165k (30% of £550k) is required to secure the place.

We are reaching out to forward thinking entrepreneurs who see the beauty and potential.

Timing

Someone put £5k non-refundable deposit and property was taken off the market

Called on 30 January 2017 and the transaction didn't succed - real estate told me a story about a guy and his daughter - she wanted some property in Spain.

There are some equity crowdfunding platforms in place - crowdcube, seedrs - and we may want to use them once we secure the property. Because Thorn Island is on the market and receives massive media attention there is not enough time to go "conventional" route... We need to secure the property first and then raise the remaining money for refurbishment.

We need to raise the deposit in the next couple of days. It is opportunity of the lifetime and is not going to last forever.
